## Project Topic
Our team has chosen **Sports Analytics: Imdol Cricket Club** as the project topic. The focus is to analyze real-world cricket performance data of the Imdol Cricket Club players and matches to understand patterns, trends, and factors influencing player and team outcomes.

## Problem Statement
The problem statement for our project is:  
*Our team aims to analyze real-world cricket data from Imdol Cricket Club to identify patterns and relationships that affect player performance and match outcomes. We will apply statistical modeling and predictive techniques, including Linear Regression, ANOVA, and Logistic Regression, to generate actionable insights and support data-driven decision-making for the club.*

## Planned Analysis
Our planned analysis will follow these steps:  

1. **Data Cleaning and Preprocessing** ‚Äì Handle missing values, remove duplicates, detect and manage outliers, and convert cricket match and player data into a structured format suitable for analysis.  
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** ‚Äì Use descriptive statistics and visualizations (scatter plots, histograms, box plots) to understand trends, distributions, and relationships between players‚Äô performance metrics, match outcomes, and team statistics.  
3. **Statistical Modeling** ‚Äì Apply Linear Regression to predict numerical outcomes (e.g., runs scored, wickets taken), Logistic Regression to predict categorical outcomes (e.g., win/loss), and ANOVA to test for significant differences between players or matches.  
4. **Visualization and Interpretation** ‚Äì Present findings through visualizations and interpret results to provide actionable insights on player performance and strategies for Imdol Cricket Club.  
5. **Reporting** ‚Äì Compile all methods, results, insights, and recommendations into a comprehensive report documenting the analysis process for the club.
